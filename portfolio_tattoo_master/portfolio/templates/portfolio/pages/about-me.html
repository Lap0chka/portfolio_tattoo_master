{% trans "What can I tell you about me?" %}

Please allow me to introduce myself. My name is Rima, and I am a tattoo artist with over five years of experience in the industry. Throughout my journey, I have had the privilege of creating numerous tattoos that have left a lasting impression on my clients. It's not just a profession for me; it's a passion that fuels my creativity and allows me to express myself artistically.
Aside from my love for tattooing, I have a deep affection for travel and exploration. Roaming the world has not only broadened my horizons but has also inspired my artistic vision. Each destination brings new experiences and perspectives that I infuse into my craft, resulting in unique and diverse creations.
Moreover, I am an avid animal lover. Animals have always held a special place in my heart, and their beauty and grace often find their way into my designs. Whether it's capturing the elegance of a feline or the strength of a wolf, I strive to honor these magnificent creatures through my art.
In essence, I am Rima – a dedicated tattoo artist, a passionate traveler, and a devoted animal enthusiast. I look forward to sharing my artistic journey with you and creating meaningful tattoos that reflect your individuality and spirit. Thank you for allowing me the opportunity to introduce myself.
