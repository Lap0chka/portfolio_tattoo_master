{% trans "What can I tell you about me?" %}

{% blocktrans %} My name is Rimma, and I have been a tattoo artist for over 5 years. I specialize in graphic, fine line, and botanical tattoo styles.
I graduated from art school and continue to practice drawing to this day. Art has always been a huge part of my life, and tattooing allows me to bring meaningful designs to people. For me, a tattoo is not just an image—it is an important story, a way to express emotions, memories, or milestones. That’s why I focus on working closely with my clients to understand their wishes and ideas, ensuring that every piece is unique and personal. I love working with details and creating delicate, elegant designs.
I easily connect with people, so during my tattoo sessions, you will feel comfortable—no awkwardness or fear. I always strive to create a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere where you can enjoy the process. I look forward to meeting you and creating something special together! {% endblocktrans %}
